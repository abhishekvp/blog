Mozilla has built a Mozillians’ Monument near its San Francisco Office Space, with the names of the contributors who have made significant contributions to Mozilla. It is indeed a great honor for me to have my name on it.

This picture below is a high resolution image of Front Side of the Monument, with my name on it! This gesture by Mozilla for its contributors gives me immense pleasure and pride! Thank You Mozilla ! :D
